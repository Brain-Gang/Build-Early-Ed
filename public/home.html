Head Start Programs

Head Start is a federally funded early childhood education program providing wrap-around support services to children and families. If you are currently pregnant or have children from birth to age 5, learn more about the services offered below.

Prenatal Support

Early Head Start programs offer prenatal services and follow-up.

Early Learning and Development

Head Start fosters school readiness and social emotional well-being to support a smooth transition to kindergarten.

Health and Wellness

Head Start provides health screenings, nutritious meals, and connects families to medical, dental, and mental health services.

Family Wellbeing

Parents and families are offered services to achieve housing stability, continued education, and financial security.
