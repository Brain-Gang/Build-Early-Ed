Connect to Local Organizations

New Mexico has a network of organizations committed to providing services for children and families. Connect with local organizations listed below to learn more about the resources located near you.

Cradle to Career Policy Institute

The Cradle to Career Policy Institute helps you search for early education and care resources.

Visit CCPI
Anna Age Eight Institute

The Anna Age Eight Institute organizes community leaders to create a system of care for youth.

Visit AAEI
Children Youth and Families Department

The Children Youth and Families Department connects residents to child care, foster care, and health resources.

Visit CYFD
Early Childhood Development Partnership

The Early Childhood Development Partnership increases awareness and advocacy for investments in early childhood care and education.

Visit NMECDP
