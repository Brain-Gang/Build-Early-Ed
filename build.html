Build a Head Start

For communities under resourced by Head Start, we encourage families to connect with local organizations to access alternative programs and services. Communities can also jumpstart the application process to apply for federal funding to build a Head Start with eligible organizations.

Community Building Forum

Build Early Ed is creating a platform to bridge the gap between early childhood organizations, teachers, advocates, and families. This messaging platform streamlines access to mentorship, job opportunities, resources, and community surveys. Ultimately, we aim to foster dialogue in the community to build the continuum of care for youth together.
